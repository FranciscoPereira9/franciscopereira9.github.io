## A Warm Welcome

Welcome to yet another personal blog page. Well, this one will follow the journey of a young engineer and data scientist who is about to graduate and will walk into the realms of industry.



I will use this space to share my views and thoughts on pretty much anything I believe it's worth putting up here. My idea is that this will be a space for personal growth, where I will be able to share my ideas with the world. I believe that openly sharing ideas, helps the world progress and helps me progress. This will force me to structure my thoughts and will create opportunities to engage in productive discussions that will allow me get different perspectives on the world. 



I hope you enjoy my posts, and feel free to reach me anytime.
